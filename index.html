About Pandit Anand Sulakhe

Pandit Anand Sulakhe is the Head Priest of Shri Sai DwarkaMai Dham Temple — one of the most respected Sai temples in the GTA. Unlike many part-time or occasional priests, Panditji comes from a second-generation brahmin priest family, where the sacred tradition of Vedic rituals has been passed down with full dedication and devotion.

With over 15 years of professional experience performing poojas, Panditji brings unmatched authenticity, deep scriptural knowledge and heartfelt warmth to every ceremony. Priesthood is not a side profession for him — it is his lifelong calling and his family's sacred legacy.

He is fluent in Hindi, Marathi and English, making every ceremony accessible and meaningful for families across the GTA.
