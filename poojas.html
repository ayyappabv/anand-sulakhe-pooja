Each ritual is a divine conversation between you and the universe. Performed with authentic Vedic mantras, proper vidhi and heartfelt devotion by Pandit Anand Sulakhe.

Satyanarayan Pooja

सत्यनारायण पूजा • सत्यनारायण पूजा
Prosperity Health Family Wellbeing

The Satyanarayan Pooja is one of the most beloved and widely performed Hindu rituals, dedicated to Lord Vishnu in his form as Satyanarayan — the Lord of Truth. Families perform this pooja to express gratitude for blessings received and to invite prosperity, good health and happiness into their homes.

It is traditionally performed on auspicious occasions such as housewarming, marriage anniversaries, births, business openings, or simply on a full moon day (Purnima). The katha (sacred story) narrated during the pooja teaches the virtues of devotion, honesty and surrender to the divine.

Vastu Shanti Pooja

वास्तु शांती पूजा • वास्तु शान्ति पूजा
Harmony Positive Energy Peace

Vastu Shanti is a sacred ritual performed to purify a home or workspace and align it with the natural and cosmic forces of the universe. According to Vastu Shastra — the ancient Indian science of architecture — every space holds energy. When that energy is imbalanced, families may experience unrest, financial difficulties or health problems.

The Vastu Shanti pooja cleanses these negative energies through Vedic mantras, homam (fire ritual) and worship of the Vastu Purusha — the deity of the space. It brings peace, harmony and positive vibrations to the home, ensuring the well-being of all who live there.

Housewarming / Gruha Pravesh Pooja

गृहप्रवेश पूजा • गृहप्रवेश पूजा
New Beginnings Divine Blessings

Moving into a new home is one of life's most significant milestones. The Gruha Pravesh pooja is performed before entering a newly built or purchased home for the first time, to invite divine blessings and ensure the space is spiritually cleansed and consecrated.

The ritual involves lighting a sacred fire (havan), performing Ganesh pooja, Lakshmi pooja and Vastu puja, followed by the family entering the home with a lit lamp and boiling of milk — all auspicious signs of abundance and new beginnings.

Gruha Shanti Pooja

गृह शांती पूजा • गृह शान्ति पूजा
Restoration Calm & Balance

Gruha Shanti is performed when a household is experiencing prolonged disturbance, conflicts, ill health, financial struggles or negative energy. It is a deeply purifying ritual that restores peace and positive harmony within the family and the home environment.

The ceremony involves recitation of specific shanti mantras, havan, and prayers to Navagrahas (nine planets) to neutralize any adverse planetary influences that may be affecting the household.

Ganesh Pooja

गणेश पूजा • गणेश पूजा
Remove Obstacles Auspicious Start

Lord Ganesha is worshipped at the beginning of every important undertaking — be it a new home, a business venture, a wedding or any celebration. He is the remover of obstacles (Vighnaharta) and the deity of wisdom and new beginnings.

Ganesh Pooja is performed to seek his blessings before any major event, ensuring that the path ahead is clear, auspicious and free of hurdles. It is a joyful and energizing ritual loved by families of all ages.

Durga / Lakshmi Pooja

दुर्गा / लक्ष्मी पूजा • दुर्गा / लक्ष्मी पूजा
Strength Abundance Protection

Goddess Durga is the divine embodiment of shakti (power) and protection, worshipped especially during Navratri and times when families seek her strength and courage. Goddess Lakshmi is the deity of wealth, fortune and auspiciousness, invoked for prosperity and abundance in all aspects of life.

These poojas can be performed on special occasions, festivals, or any time a family wishes to invoke the divine feminine energy for blessings of strength, protection and lasting prosperity.
